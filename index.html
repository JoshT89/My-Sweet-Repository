My Coding Journey

by Joshua Turner

I am 33 years old and was not happy with my working life, it made me anxious and stressed turning my free weekends into recovery time rather than relaxation time.

My journey started while working my 9-6 job monday to friday in medical recruitment. I have a friend who taught himself to code and is now able to travel the world while working and earning very good money.

I knew I needed to make a change and soon!!

My first step was reseraching who i should learn coding with, and Code Academy was reffered to by my friend I mentioned earlier. Code academy seemed a very good choice as it is recognised by some of the largest computer companies in the world and had many great reviews. I decided to jump stright in and paid for the pro course to become a Front End Egineer. Handing in my notice was an incredible feeling already making me feel happier and a sense of freedom and excitment.
